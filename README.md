Eorenda Capital — Live Track Record

Overview

Eorenda is a systematic capital deployment engine that allocates into concentrated equity beta exposure under specific regime conditions and remains in cash otherwise. The system operates on a fixed daily schedule and executes trades automatically without discretionary intervention.

Rules

All trades are generated and executed by the system.
No manual overrides are permitted.
The system operates daily at fixed times.

Capital
